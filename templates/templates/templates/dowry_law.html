Dowry Law in India

Dowry is a criminal offence under the Dowry Prohibition Act, 1961. Asking for, giving, or accepting dowry is illegal.

Punishment

  • Imprisonment up to 5 years
  • Fine of ₹15,000 or the value of dowry

Related IPC Sections

  • IPC Section 498A – Cruelty by husband or relatives
  • IPC Section 304B – Dowry death

Marriage is about respect, not money.
